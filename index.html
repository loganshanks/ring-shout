Ring Shout Portal

Ring Shout is a spell. Ring Shout is a divination. Ring Shout is a ritual. Crafted by Playing in Excess, The Ring Shout Portal serves as a technology of liberation—conjuring a world for transnational and intergenerational art and community work. Enter your information to join Ring Shout, a community of students, scholars, writers, activists, organizers, curators, artists, directors, healers, teachers, cosmetologists, cultural workers, freelance multi-hyphenated Black creatives who wish to produce artistic projects in ethical community spaces. You will be added to our newsletter that will share content and resources related to expanding your art and community practice such as (but not limited to)—grants/residences, co-creative opportunities, workshops, and community gatherings.
